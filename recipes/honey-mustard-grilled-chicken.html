Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup Dijon Mustard
  • 1/4 cup Honey
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon steak sauce
  • 4 skinless, boenless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill for medium heat.
  2. Lightly oil the grill grate. Grill chicken over indirect heat for 18 to 20 minutes, turning occasionally, or until juices run clear. Baste occasionally with the reserved sauce during the last 10 minutes. Watch carefully to prevent burning!In a shallow bowl, mix the mustard, honey, mayonnaise, and steak sauce.
